Curriculum

Module-by-Module Breakdown

Every module is built around live demonstration and active participation. No one sits and watches for 4 hours.

Module 01
AI Literacy. What It Is, What It Is Not, and Why It Matters Now
60 Minutes
Objective

Every participant understands what AI actually is, how it fits their business, and why the window to act early is right now.

Topics Covered
  • What AI is and what it is not. Kill the sci-fi myths in the first 5 minutes. We cover the actual definition, how large language models work without the jargon, and why AI in 2025 is fundamentally different from anything that came before.
  • How family businesses in retail, manufacturing, and services are already using AI today. Real examples, real numbers, real time saved. Not case studies from Silicon Valley. Examples from businesses that look like theirs.
  • The shift from AI as a tool to AI as a team member who never sleeps. What changes when you stop using AI occasionally and start delegating to it systematically.
  • ChatGPT vs Claude vs Gemini vs Grok vs Perplexity. A live side-by-side comparison on the same task so the room can see the difference, not just hear about it.
  • The only mindset shift that matters. Stop typing queries. Start giving assignments. Why this one change determines whether AI saves you 30 minutes a week or 30 hours.
Outcome
Participants understand the real-world stakes, can tell the difference between the major AI tools, and know exactly which one fits their business type and why.
Module 02
Prompt Engineering. The Skill Nobody Taught You
60 Minutes
Objective

Participants move from vague one-liners to structured prompts that get finished, usable work on the first try.

Topics Covered
  • Why bad prompts waste time and how to spot them instantly. We show the 5 most common prompting mistakes business owners make and what each one costs in time and output quality.
  • The RCTF framework: Role, Context, Task, Format. The one structure that works across every business use case, every tool, and every department. Once you know this, you never prompt blindly again.
  • Live demo: Same business task, 3 different prompts, 3 wildly different outputs. The room votes on the winner and we break down exactly why it won.
  • Hands-on prompting round: every participant writes a prompt for a real task from their own business. Rishi reviews live, corrects in real time, and shows the improved output on screen.
  • Business prompts that actually get used: vendor negotiation emails, staff briefings, sales pitch scripts, Instagram captions, WhatsApp broadcasts, and investor summaries. Built live, not just described.
  • The one-shot vs multi-shot approach. When to refine the prompt vs when to start over. How to have a conversation with AI instead of shouting instructions at it.
Outcome
Participants leave with the RCTF framework memorised, at least one business prompt they built themselves during the session, and the ability to get usable output from any AI tool on the first or second try.
Module 03
GenAI for Your Business. Real Use Cases, Not Theory
60 Minutes
Objective

Every participant walks away with AI use cases mapped to their specific business function and a clear picture of what AI can do across every major area of their work.

Topics Covered
  • Content and marketing at speed: product descriptions, Instagram captions, WhatsApp broadcast messages, sales pitches, email newsletters, and ad copy. All built live during the session using real business scenarios from the room.
  • Visual content without a designer: how to use AI image and video tools to create product visuals, presentation decks, explainer videos, and branded content in minutes, not days.
  • Operations and sales support: SOP drafting, vendor comparison emails, follow-up templates, objection handling scripts, and meeting summaries. Practical outputs a business can use the same week.
  • Common mistakes every business owner makes with AI: hallucinations and how to catch them, the copy-paste trap, what data should never go into a public AI tool, and a 60-second review habit that prevents 90 percent of costly errors.
  • Rapid-fire live build: someone from the audience names a real business problem. Rishi builds the AI output on screen in 90 seconds. No prep. No script.
  • A curated walkthrough of the best AI tools available today by category: content, visuals, research, communication, and productivity. Not a list. A live demo of each one on a real task so the room knows what to reach for and when.
Outcome
Participants identify their top three time-draining tasks, leave with AI-generated outputs for at least one of them built during the session, and know exactly which tool to use for every category of work in their business.
Module 04
Claude Masterclass. The Full Ecosystem Walkthrough
60 Minutes
Objective

Participants go deep into Claude as a business operating system, covering why it wins over other tools and how to set it up to work for their specific business.

Topics Covered
  • Claude vs ChatGPT vs Gemini. A side-by-side comparison on memory, accuracy, file handling, and business-grade output quality.
  • Claude Projects and Memory setup. How to make Claude remember your business, your tone, and your preferences across every conversation.
  • Skills. How to build reusable instruction sets so Claude executes recurring tasks the same way every single time.
  • Claude for specific business workflows: content calendars, vendor communications, financial summaries, and team briefings built end to end.
  • Live build: Rishi sets up a working Claude workflow for a business type from the room. Start to finish, in real time.
Outcome
Participants leave with Claude configured for their business and at least one reusable workflow ready to run from Day 1.
